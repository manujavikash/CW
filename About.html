About Manuja

Manuja Vikash is a dedicated BSc (Hons) Computer Science student at IIT, driven by a deep passion for technology and innovation. He actively enhances his understanding of computing through a focus on programming, algorithms, and the latest industry trends. Recognized for his analytical skills and problem-solving abilities, Manuja is committed to advancing his expertise in the dynamic field of computer science.


In addition to his academic pursuits, Manuja is a prominent drummer with the Ravibandhu-Samanthi Drum Ensemble, where he also teaches. As the percussionist for IIT's H36 band as well, he has performed at a variety of events, showcasing his musical talents.


Manuja also has a keen interest in graphic design, dedicating his free time to exploring current trends and honing his skills in Photoshop and Illustrator.
